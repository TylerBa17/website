Job Specifications

Job Summary: As a Web Developer, you will be responsible for designing, coding, and modifying websites and web applications to meet the needs of our clients. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams, including designers, project managers, and other developers, to create user-friendly and visually appealing web experiences. You will also be involved in maintaining and enhancing existing websites and troubleshooting any issues that arise. The ideal candidate will have a strong passion for web development, a solid foundation in front-end and back-end technologies, and a desire to stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies.
Responsibilities:

Collaborate with designers and project managers to understand client requirements and translate them into functional web applications. Develop user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies. Write efficient, reusable, and well-documented code. Implement and customize content management systems (CMS) such as WordPress, Drupal, or Joomla. Integrate third-party APIs, plugins, and libraries to enhance website functionality. Optimize websites for maximum speed and scalability. Conduct thorough testing and debugging to ensure seamless user experience across multiple browsers and devices. Maintain and update existing websites, making necessary changes and improvements. Stay up-to-date with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in web development. Collaborate with team members to troubleshoot and resolve website issues. Provide technical support and guidance to clients and internal stakeholders.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field (or equivalent experience). Proven experience as a Web Developer, preferably in a professional setting. Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other front-end web technologies. Strong understanding of responsive web design principles and cross-browser compatibility. Experience with at least one back-end programming language (e.g., PHP, Python, Ruby, or Java) and related frameworks. Familiarity with content management systems (CMS) and e-commerce platforms. Knowledge of database systems (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) and ability to write efficient queries. Understanding of web security best practices and experience implementing security measures. Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills. Strong attention to detail and ability to work independently or as part of a team. Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with mobile app development (iOS, Android). Knowledge of version control systems (e.g., Git, SVN). Familiarity with front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js). Understanding of SEO principles and best practices. Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure) and server management. Graphic design skills and proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator) is a plus. We offer competitive compensation and benefits packages, including opportunities for professional development and growth. If you are passionate about web development, enjoy working in a collaborative environment, and want to contribute to exciting projects, we would love to hear from you.

To apply, please submit your resume, portfolio showcasing your web development work, and any relevant links or projects you have contributed to.
